## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Keyword stuff (using keywords unnaturally)
- Buy low-quality backlinks
- Duplicate content across pages
- Hide text or misleading content
- Forget to update business info consistently
- Ignore customer reviews
- Only optimize for one city

✅ **Do:**
- Write naturally for humans first
- Quality over quantity
- Build one-way backlinks through content
- Be transparent and honest
- Keep all business info consistent
- Actively manage reviews
- Optimize for each city/service
